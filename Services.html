SUNDAY

— BAKERY CHARITY —

ABOUT

Liviru's Delights is providing a charity service every Sunday to eldery homes and orphanages to bake and provide treats which everyone loves!

This is a voluntary program and anyone with the capability to bake is invited to join and provide a helping hand to share our delights to the community outside.

WHAT YOU GAIN FOR YOUR SERVICE

  • A valid certification provided by the Ministry of Food.
  • A chance to explore various baking methods and strategies.
  • Improve the inner baking skills within you.
  • Friends
